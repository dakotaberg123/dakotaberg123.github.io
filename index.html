About Me

I am a freelance graphic designer & entrepreneur from Spokane, Washington. I consider my design style to be clean, iconic, unique and memorable. I have worked with a variety of clients in a wide array of industries. Design may be my job, but innovation is my passion. I like to think outside of the proverbial box and deliver my clients solutions that they may not have expected. With that said, my job is not complete until my client is completely satisfied. If you are interested in working with me, I am currently accepting freelance design projects.
